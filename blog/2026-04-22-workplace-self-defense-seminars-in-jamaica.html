Understanding Workplace Self Defense

Workplace self-defense is the proactive approach to preparing individuals for potential threats or violence at work. In today's world, where workplace incidents are sadly not uncommon, being equipped with the right tools and knowledge is crucial. Unlike traditional self-defense training, workplace self-defense focuses on scenarios specific to professional settings, addressing issues like conflict resolution and safety protocols.
