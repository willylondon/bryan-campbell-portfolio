The Executive Profile

Distinguished Fitness Consultant, Grandmaster, and Athletic Pioneer with over 50 years of experience transforming physical and mental health. A visionary founder of multiple organizations including the Kukibo Martial Arts and Fitness Association and the Universal Self Defense Organization.

Renowned for bridging the gap between traditional martial arts discipline and modern fitness technology. Proven track record in elite coaching, having trained national teams (Sunshine Girls), law enforcement (JCF), and celebrity clients. Dedicated to empowering individuals through accessible, lifestyle-oriented fitness and self-defense strategies.

Honors & Affiliations

  • Promoted to 4th Dan in Bas-I Kyokushin by SOKE Newton James.
  • Inducted into the Bas-I Kyokushin International Martial Arts Global Hall of Fame.
  • Inducted in the World Karate Masters Federation.
  • Honorary Member of the Broward County Sheriff's Office.
  • Member of the School of Vital Pressure Points Police Tactical Self Defense.
  • Provides seminars and workshops in Self Defense, Personal Protection, Health & Fitness, and Fundamentals of Cheerleading.
