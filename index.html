About Grandmaster Bryan Campbell

Founder of Kukibo Martial Arts, Distinguished Fitness Consultant, and Athletic Pioneer with over 40 years of experience. Grandmaster Campbell has dedicated his career to building stronger individuals and communities through martial arts, self-defense, and discipline-based fitness in Kingston, Jamaica.

Honors & Affiliations

  • Founder: Kukibo Martial Arts and Fitness Association (1987)
  • Executive Member: BAS-I KYOKUSHIN GLOBAL COUNCIL
  • Consultant: Caribbean Security Academy
  • Champion: Jamaica's First National Aerobics Champion (1990)
  • Awarded: Instructor & Coach of the Millennium (2000)
  • Inducted: Bas-I Kyokushin Hall of Fame
  • 4th Dan: Bas-I Kyokushin
